Past Employers

This is a page that shows my past employers and a brief description of my time there.

BD Diabetes Care

At BD, I worked in the Advanced Technology Development team coming up with and testing medical device concepts. My team was in charge of competitive intelligence, filing patents, brainstorming, and prototyping. In addition, I contributed to building up the mechanical design of a Type II diabetic insulin pump.

PV Pure

My first job out of college, I worked at a solar-powered water filtration system company, PV Pure. I built the mechanical design of water filtration systems that were sent to Mexican villages in the Sonora region.

EPIC Boston University

EPIC stands for the Engineering Product Innovation Center. Here, I helped students machine and prototype their school projects using safe and proper machining practices. I was trained in NC Milling, Lasercutting, the lathe, and 3D Printing.

ANDESITE

ADESITE Satellite Lab worked on making microsatellites to launch into space. I worked on the coding the motherboard and helped with some of the machining. The payload was an experimental mesh network to collect space weather data without losing any nodes.

Mansfield Parks and Rec

My humble beginnings. My first job I worked as a tennis instructor for a Parks and Recreation department in Mansfield MA.
