Solar Helmet

This is a Solar Helmet I created when I rode my bike through the streets of Boston. I had a bunch of Solar Leafletts hanging around, so I put them on a acrylic backing and put a protective coating of two-part epoxy. The point was to trickle charge batteries that were powering lights lining the edge of my helmet, as to not get hit by Boston drivers.
