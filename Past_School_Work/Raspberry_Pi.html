Heat Transfer of Raspberry Pi

This is a report I did with a team mate on the subject of various cooling situations of a raspberry pi CPU. The various situations investigated were a plain CPU chip, a Fan Cooled CPU chip, a CPU chip with a fin, and a CPU Chip with a fin and fan cooled. A Comsol simulation was made to verify the data collected from the experiments. All of this is shown in the report.
