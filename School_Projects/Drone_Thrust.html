About Me

This is a Drone Thrust sensor I developed for research at the robotics laboratory on the Boston University campus. The concept is that the structure would all be on a strain gauge embedded into a Wheatstone bridge. Any thrust that the drone produces would be measured by the strain gauge. This setup was meant for a laboratory exercise that would teach Dynamics students about Newton's 3rd Law using drones.
