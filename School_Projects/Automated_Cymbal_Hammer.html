Cymbal Hammer

For a class called Electromechanical Design, the final project was to do a preliminary design of a automated cymbal hammer. This system would take blank cymbals, and put dimples into the surface based on the desired surface scatter of said dimples. The purpose of the dimples is to create a higher quality sound for the symbals. Above shows a gif of how the system works.
