About me

I'm a mechanical engineer by trade, but I also have experience in software, electrical, and biomedical engineering. My wide breadth of experience allows me consider many different engineering disciplines when I'm working.

Undergrad

I obtained my BS in Mechanical Engineering at Boston University. During this time, I worked at the EPIC Center on campus where I learned about machining and other manufacturing techniques. I also worked at the Drone Research Laboratory and the ANDESITE Satellite Lab on campus.

Graduate Program

After working for a few years, I obtained my MS in Mechanical Engineering from Northeastern with a certificate in Engineering Leadership fron the Gordon Institute of Engineering Leadership. At Northeastern, I further honed my technical knowledge in Mechanical Engineering. Furthermore, at the Gordon Institute of Engineering Leadership, I learned how to use my engineering skills to best lead a high performing team and how to excel in a modern day organization.

Eagle Scout

Back in High School, I earned my Eagle Scout Rank during my time in the Boy Scouts of America. To do this, I had to show a comittment to my community through hundreds of hours of volunteer work. In addition, I had to plan and execute an Eagle Scout Project in service of my local community.

Hobbies

In my freetime, I love to hike and play tennis to stay active. I also volunteer at the Boys and Girls club to help teach STEM concepts to underprivileged communities.
